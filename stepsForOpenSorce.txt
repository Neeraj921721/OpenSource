Requirements:
1. You should be a College Student with any branch/stream.
2. You should know a programming language in deep.

Steps:
1. Go to any organisation or go to explore option on github
2. Search for your favourite topic.
3. Choose the field of your choice and read the readme file of that repo
4. Click on the open issues tab and choose which issue you want to solve.
5. Request for the issue to assign to you.
6. FCFS is the order of assigning issue.
7. Work on the issue to resolve and look out for any commnts/suggestions from other contributers or maintainers.
8. Keep trying to make the problem to resolve in best possible way.
9. After issue is resolved completely, your solution will merge into main code.
